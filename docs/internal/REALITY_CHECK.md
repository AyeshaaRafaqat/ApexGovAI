### ✅ YES For: Pre-Inspection Triage (The Real Wedge)
**This is your YC pitch:**  
> "We don't replace inspectors. We help them prioritize."

**Real World Workflow:**
1. Lahore has 50,000 buildings needing inspection
2. LDA has 20 inspectors (reality)
3. Current system: Inspect sequentially, miss 80% of critical cases
4. **ApexGov AI**: Citizens upload photos → AI flags "High Risk" buildings → Inspectors visit those FIRST

**This is defensible because:**
- You're a decision support tool, not the decision maker
- Risk tolerance is lower (false positives are okay if real inspector validates)
- Government can pilot this without changing laws
